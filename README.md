# Focumon for Anki

**Focumon for Anki** brings the gamified focus experience of [Focumon](https://www.focumon.com) directly into your Anki workflow. Collect monsters, level up, and stay motivated to review your flashcards!

## Features

*   **Focumon Window**: Access the full Focumon web application within Anki. manage your monsters, battle, and explore without leaving your study environment.
*   **Deck Widget**: A sleek, customizable widget on your Anki Deck Browser that displays your current trainer stats, active Focumon, and progress.
*   **Real-time Stats**: View your Trainer Level, Focumon Level, and Focudex progress at a glance.
*   **Seamless Integration**: Designed to look and feel like a native part of Anki, with support for both Light and Dark modes.

## Getting Started

### 1. Account Setup
To get the most out of this add-on, you need a Focumon account.
1.  Open the add-on menu and launch the **Focumon Window**.
2.  Login or Create an Account (use email login for the best experience).
3.  *Tip: If you need an account, check the Instructions dialog in the add-on for an invite link!*

### 2. User Pairing
Link your Focumon account to Anki to see your stats in the widget.
1.  Go to **Tools > Focumon > Settings** (or click the Gear icon on the Deck Widget).
2.  In the **Profile** section, enter your **Focumon Username**.
    *   *You can find your username on your Focumon Profile page by hovering over your character.*
3.  Click **Save**. Your stats should now appear in the Deck Widget!

## Components

### The Deck Widget
Located on your main Deck Browser screen, this widget gives you a quick summary of your journey.
*   **Visuals**: Displays your Trainer sprite and your current partner Focumon.
*   **Stats**: Shows your current Trainer Level and Focudex completion.
*   **Quick Actions**:
    *   **Settings (Gear Icon)**: Open the configuration menu.
    *   **Refresh (Arrows Icon)**: Reload the widget and stats if they look outdated.
    *   **Open Focumon (Gamepad Icon)**: Launch the full Focumon Window.

### Focumon Window
The main interface for interacting with the game.
*   Accessible via **Tools > Focumon > Open Focumon** or the Gamepad icon on the widget.
*   Browse your collection, battle, and manage your account.
*   **Persistent Login**: The window keeps you logged in so you're always ready to go.

### Settings

*   **Hide Widget**: Toggle this if you want to hide the Deck Widget from your main screen.
*   **Profile**: Update your linked username to sync stats.
*   **Support**: options to Report Bugs or Donate to the project.

## Troubleshooting

*   **Widget not updating?** Try clicking the **Refresh** button (arrows icon) on the widget.
*   **Visual Glitches?** If the Focumon Window looks strange, try closing and reopening it, or use the Refresh button.
*   **Stats not found?** Double-check your username in Settings. Ensure it matches your Focumon profile exactly.
